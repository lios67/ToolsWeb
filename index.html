Video Sever

Video Sever lets users split a video into its individual frames and save all of the frames to a png and zip that will be downloaded when the task is completed.

  • For the best results use videos with less than 10MB
  • Note that using any video > 10 MB could be done but it may take time
  • It will automatically download the zip of png files.
